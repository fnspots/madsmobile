Season 6 "Take Your Heart": Persona 5 Royal Arrives in COD Mobile

Publicado: 2026-07-02

Call of Duty: Mobile's Season 6, Take Your Heart, went live on July 1 at 5PM PT with one of the year's biggest collaborations: Persona 5 Royal. The Operator Series Armory includes Cipher – Joker, Scylla – Panther, Alias – Queen, and Urban Tracker – Violet, each with unique lobby entrances and mask accessories. A matching Weapon Series Armory adds four Legendary blueprints themed after the Phantom Thieves: LK24 - Arsène, QQ9 - Carmen, Type 63 - Johanna, and RAM-7 - Cendrillon. On the gameplay side, a Plunder Treasure Hunt event transforms parts of Isolated into Persona-styled domains where players fight Shadow enemies for cash and mask buffs, and a new Throwable Frenzy multiplayer mode puts the Combat Axe front and center. The season also introduced the FSS Hurricane SMG, a VTOL Jet scorestreak, and a new Battle Pass.
