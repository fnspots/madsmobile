Free Fire

Garena · 2017

Also known as: Garena Free Fire, FF, Free Fire MAX

About

Garena Free Fire is a battle royale shooter that launched globally in 2017 and became one of the most-downloaded mobile games in the world, with especially large communities across Latin America, India (as Free Fire MAX) and Southeast Asia. Matches drop 50 players onto a shrinking island, and the game is built around ability-based characters (Active and Passive skills) layered on top of a smaller, faster weapon sandbox than most mobile shooters. This page is refreshed as new characters, weapons and balance patches land.

Current Meta

Last verified: 2026-07-12

Current top-tier picks: M1887 leads all weapons for close-range eliminations, with MP40 right behind it as the highest-DPS rush SMG. Groza remains the best all-around assault rifle at any range, and Woodpecker is the top precision rifle for armor-penetrating headshots. On the character side, Alok, Chrono and K form the core of nearly every competitive lineup thanks to their Active abilities, while Jota and Hayato are close to mandatory Passive picks for aggressive, rush-focused squads.
