Season 6 Weapon Balance: Switchblade X9 Takes the Throne, SO-14 Nerfed

Published: 2026-07-05

Season 6's balance pass reshuffled the Call of Duty: Mobile meta more than any patch so far in 2026. The Switchblade X9 received buffs that pushed it to the top of the SMG tier list, edging out the long-reliable VMP and QQ9. Assault rifles saw a bigger shake-up: the SO-14, which had dominated multiple prior seasons, was hit with a notable nerf, opening the door for the newly-strong BAL-27 to take over as the best all-around AR for mid-to-long range fights. Shotguns remain close-quarters kings, with HS0405 holding steady and the newly-added VLK Rogue debuting straight into S-tier thanks to its magazine size and mobility. Locus continues to lead sniper play, and Type 63 remains the go-to two-shot marksman rifle. As always, treat tier lists as a snapshot — recheck after each balance patch.
