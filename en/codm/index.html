Call of Duty: Mobile

Activision / Garena / Tencent (TiMi Studios) · 2019

Also known as: COD Mobile, CODM, Call of Duty Mobile

Developer: TiMi Studio Group (Tencent)Publisher: Activision / Garena / Tencent (TiMi Studios)Official Website: www.callofduty.com/mobile

Last verified: 2026-07-12

Season 6 — Take Your Heart (Persona 5 Royal collaboration) is live as of July 2026. Current class leaders: Switchblade X9 tops the SMG tier list after a Season 6 buff, with VMP and QQ9 close behind. BAL-27 leads Assault Rifles for mid-to-long range. Type 63 remains the top two-shot Marksman Rifle. HS0405 and the newly-added VLK Rogue are the top Shotguns, and Locus is the best Sniper this season. The SO-14 received a notable nerf this patch after dominating prior seasons.

Call of Duty: Mobile launched globally in October 2019, published by Activision and developed by TiMi Studio Group (Tencent). It brings the core Call of Duty multiplayer and Battle Royale experience to phones, blending maps, weapons, and operators from across the console franchise. The game runs on seasonal updates (roughly every 4-6 weeks) that introduce new weapons, operator skins, ranked resets, and rotating collaboration events. This page is refreshed as each season lands to keep the meta and roster current.
