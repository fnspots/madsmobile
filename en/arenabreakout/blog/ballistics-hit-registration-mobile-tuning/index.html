Arena Breakout Mobile: Ballistics and Hit Registration Tuned for Touch Controls

Published: 2026-07-04

Arena Breakout doesn't forgive mistakes, and on mobile a bad hit registration sends you straight back to the lobby with nothing to show for your loadout. The latest technical passes focus on tightening bullet penetration against armor and body hitboxes so the game's extreme realism translates cleanly to touch controls and mobile connections. The core penetration math against armor level has been refined for limb hits: fragmentation damage and the mobility penalty from a destroyed limb now calculate more precisely in real time, so getting hit in the legs with fragmentation rounds produces an immediate but gradual slow instead of the desync spikes that used to make it feel like your character had frozen in place. Arm hitboxes while aiming down sights were also corrected: arms used to absorb shots that should have logically hit the chest rig, skewing duel outcomes; the ballistic calculation now prioritizes the bullet's real trajectory, letting it pass through an arm to reach torso armor if it still carries enough kinetic energy. Since Arena Breakout runs with almost no magnetic aim assist to preserve its difficulty, the settings menu received a finer gyroscope sensitivity curve, and matchmaking server tick-rate was improved to reduce the 'ghost bullet' effect when firing bursts at fast lateral targets.
