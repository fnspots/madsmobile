Arena Breakout

Tencent Games (MoreFun Studios), published globally via Level Infinite / Proxima Beta · 2022 (China) / 2023 (global)

Also known as: Arena Breakout, AB, 暗区突围, Arena Breakout Mobile

About

Arena Breakout is a realistic mobile extraction shooter developed by Tencent's MoreFun Studios, known for deep, authentic weapon customization inspired by the hardcore looter-shooter genre. It launched in mainland China on July 13, 2022, and globally (published via Level Infinite / Proxima Beta) on July 14, 2023, drawing over 10 million global pre-registrations. The game runs on regular content updates that rebalance weapon attachments, pricing, and ammo performance across its full arsenal. This page is refreshed as each patch lands to keep the meta current.

Current Meta

Last verified: 2026-07-12

Current S+ tier picks lead every major class: the H416 and FAL top the Assault Rifle tier for high-end competitive play, the P90 dominates SMGs as an armored-squad eraser, the AX50 is the top one-shot sniper pick, the Mk14 leads DMRs as a fully-automatic monster, and the M32 Grenade Launcher is the go-to pick for clearing buildings and squads. One tier down, the AEK-971, Groza, MP5, MPX, SR-25 and G18C round out the S-tier options across their classes.
