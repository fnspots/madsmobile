PUBG Mobile

Tencent (LightSpeed & Quantum Studio) in partnership with PUBG Studios / Krafton · 2018

Also known as: PUBG Mobile, PUBGM, PUBG: Exhilarating Battlefield

About

PUBG Mobile is a free-to-play battle royale shooter co-developed by Tencent's LightSpeed & Quantum Studio in partnership with PUBG Studios (Krafton), bringing the original PUBG: Battlegrounds experience to phones. It released in China as 'PUBG: Exhilarating Battlefield' on February 9, 2018, then launched worldwide as PUBG Mobile on March 19, 2018, going on to become one of the highest-grossing mobile games of all time. The game runs on regular content updates that rebalance weapon stats, attachments, and map rotations. This page is refreshed as each patch lands to keep the meta current.

Current Meta

Last verified: 2026-07-12

Current category leaders: the AUG tops Assault Rifles for stability and 5.56mm ammo economy, the P90 leads SMGs with extreme close-range raw DPS, the MK14 is the top DMR for high-precision damage, the AWM remains the top Sniper Rifle for massive one-shot potential, the DBS leads Shotguns for one-tap headshot priority, and the MG3 tops LMGs for sustained suppression. Recommended pairings from the current meta dashboard include AUG+MK12 for a competitive-focused loadout, Beryl M762+Mini14 for an aggressive slayer build, and M416+Mini14 as a balanced, beginner-friendly setup.
