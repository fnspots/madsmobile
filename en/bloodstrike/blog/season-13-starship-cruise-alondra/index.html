Season 13 "Starship Cruise" Arrives: Meet Alondra

Published: 2026-03-12

Blood Strike's Season 13, Starship Cruise, launched March 12, 2026. The headline addition is Alondra, an engineering prodigy from Rio de Janeiro who fights with a homemade jetpack for sustained vertical mobility and an Arc Snare gadget that creates a slowing damage zone to control fights. The Starship Cruise Strike Pass (520 Gold) is the confirmed way to unlock her. Free-to-play players aren't left out: Kainda is obtainable without spending, bundled with a permanent Butterfly Knife and a Dirt Bike skin, plus titles and Gold. On the cosmetics side, Epic-rarity skins for Emma and Jack, both in the Starship Cruise theme, round out the season's premium track.
