Blood Strike

NetEase Games · 2023 (SEA) / 2024 (global)

About

Blood Strike is a free-to-play mobile battle royale FPS from NetEase Games. It soft-launched in Southeast Asia in October 2023, then launched globally in March 2024, quickly surpassing 50 million installs within about a year. A PC version followed on Steam in March 2025. The game runs on a seasonal content cycle, introducing new Strikers (operators), weapons, and limited-time collaboration events roughly every couple of months. This page is refreshed as each season lands to keep the meta and roster current.

Current Meta

Last verified: 2026-07-12

As of mid-2026 -- in the Season 13 'Starship Cruise' era, with the ENZO Peak Pitch collaboration event running through July 19 -- the top-tier weapons are the UMP, Spear, and HK416, valued for the best time-to-kill-to-recoil ratios at close-to-mid range. The Magnum has made a major comeback as a two-shot pick, and the M1887 shotgun remains powerful even after recent nerfs. The AK47 stays a high-skill, high-reward option. One tier down, the INP9 (with armor-piercing rounds), the M82 sniper, the PP-19 Bizon, and the RPK round out the current meta.
