Blood Strike

NetEase Games · 2023 (SEA) / 2024 (global)

Developer: NetEase GamesPublisher: NetEase GamesOfficial Website: www.blood-strike.com

Last verified: 2026-07-12

As of mid-2026, the Weapons Database's confirmed Top Meta Kingpins are the UMP, Spear and HK416, prized for the best time-to-kill-to-recoil ratio at close-to-mid range. The MP7 is a fast-rising meta pick thanks to its 4.6x30mm caliber shredding armor at close range, the PKM anchors squad-level suppression after a recoil and damage buff, the M1887 retains its favorite one-shot potential despite historic nerfs, and the P90 remains the go-to pick for clearing squads without reloading thanks to its deep base magazine.

Blood Strike is a free-to-play mobile battle royale FPS from NetEase Games. It soft-launched in Southeast Asia in October 2023, then launched globally in March 2024, quickly surpassing 50 million installs within about a year. A PC version followed on Steam in March 2025. The game runs on a seasonal content cycle, introducing new Strikers (operators), weapons, and limited-time collaboration events roughly every couple of months. This page is refreshed as each season lands to keep the meta and roster current.
