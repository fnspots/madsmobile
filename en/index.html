About MADS Mobile

MADS Mobile — short for Meta, Arsenal, Data, Shooters — is a fact-checked reference database for today's biggest mobile shooters: Call of Duty Mobile, Free Fire, PUBG Mobile, Delta Force Mobile, Arena Breakout, and Blood Strike. Each game's page tracks the current weapon meta, full stat breakdowns, operator and character skins, and season-by-season news, refreshed as updates land. The goal is a single, reliable, multi-language source players and content creators can check before every match — not another rumor mill. Available in English, Chinese, French, Italian, Portuguese, Hindi, and Spanish.

MADS Mobile is a fact-checked database of weapons, characters and meta rankings for the world's top mobile shooters.

The information published on MADS Mobile is based on research, analysis and evaluation of available game data. Updates and balance changes may modify statistics and recommendations. If you find discrepancies, please contact our team. Contact: madsmobilegg@gmail.com
